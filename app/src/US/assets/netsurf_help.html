Netsurf Mobile Application


This mobile app is exclusively created for the Independent Direct Sellers of Netsurf Network doing direct selling business. It helps in below ways-

  • Increased efficiency & productivity in business operations on-the-go

  • Better connectivity with the downline teams and business prospects through smart phones phones

  • Assistance in Pay-out cycle wise target seeking and achievement

  • Smarter business analysis & progress mapping

  • Business Growth in terms of pay out


This article contains an overview and usage directions of Netsurf Mobile Application for the help of the user in managing his/her Netsurf's direct selling business easily and effectively on the go, on his/her own smartphone.


Help Topics-

A] Current business status overview:

See your status of achievements of your turnover target and Club Netsurf. Also receive a daily calling target and enter call details once done.


On logging in with your username and password to your account it displays the basic statistics of your business-

  1. Pay-out Cycle Wise Turnover Target:

  1. To be able to view the target completion and balance figure you need to tap on Set Pay-out Cycle wise Target to enter the target periodically at the beginning of every Pay-out cycle.

  2. Details of the current Pay-out cycle: Target of the Pay-out cycle - Achieved figure - Balance figure.

  3. You can see your Pay-out in Last Pay-out Cycle, team wise T.O. & Self Purchase T.O. by tapping on My Account Page link provided below.

  1. Club Netsurf Status:

  1. Target displayed along with your achievements- both team wise and Self Purchase wise- for your quick review.

  2. You can take an overview of your earlier Pay-out cycle wise Phase achievements of Club Netsurf Targets by tapping on theDetails link mentioned below.

  1. Daily Calling Target:

  1. Once you have entered your Pay-out cycle wise turnover target, your daily calling target will be automatically calculated and displayed here for every Pay-out cycle. It will be based on your club level and the Pay-out cycle of the year.

  2. You can add the details of the calls made by tapping on Enter Details link provided below.

B] Add a New Direct Seller:

Check the Pay-out earned and power teams of your active downline and add new direct seller in your network accordingly.

  • To add a new direct seller, you can tap on the first Icon, present on extreme left positioned on the upper bar of landing page.

  • You can now see a display of your entire tree of network. Every reference no. is shown in a thumbnail pattern in the tree.

  • To see the status of both teams of any individual direct seller in the tree, you can tap on the reference no. in any of the thumbnails and you get all the details of that direct seller displayed on your screen.

  • The details include the direct seller’s basic information along with the carry forward balance for you. The carry forward balance for you can denote if the person belongs to the power team of yours.

  • Going back after selecting the right reference no. where in you would like to add your new joinee, you can tap on the thumbnail (mentioning add here) itself.It leads you to another page displaying the Online Registration Form.

  • You can enter the details like your login details, Personal details & address of the new joinee and submit post accepting the terms & conditions. This completes the process of introducing a new Independent Direct Seller to the business.

C] Pay-out Cycle Comparison

Check comparison between your last Pay-out cycle and current Pay-out cycle performance both turnover wise and count wise.

  • To view the Pay-out cycle wise comparison, tap on the second icon, depicting ‘Rs’ symbol, present next to the ‘add new direct seller’ icon.

  • It displays a comparison between current and last Pay-out cycle in both receipt count and T.O. basis.

D] Online Product Shopping:

Shop all the products on your mobile phone and make online payment through IDBI bank account & get them at your doorsteps.



Shop for your choice of Netsurf products online by tapping on third icon from the left present next to the ‘Pay-out Cycle Comparison’ icon. It will collect inputs from you in below four steps-



  1. Product Selection: You can choose your products across all 4 categories. Tap on Place my order Proceed to proceed for shipping details.

  2. Shipping: Select from your previous addresses or enter a new address for dispatch. Once done, on Go to Order Confirmation

  3. Order Confirmation: You can review your order and confirm it. There is an option of editing, if needed. You can also redeem your promo code here (if any). Once done, tap on Go to Payment

  4. Payment: Make your payment via Net Banking facility of IDBI bank and this completes your online order.

E] My Region:

See area wise Pay-out cycle comparison and all regions Club Netsurf achievements in terms of the lists of actual achievers and probable achievers for each region.

  1. The Area Comparison Section opens and shows following details about your region or multiple regions in current Pay-out cycle--

  1. Pay-out Cycle wise minimum target

  2. Total Turnover till date

  3. Regular Turnover till date

  4. Self Purchase Turnover till date

  1. Another Region wise Club Netsurf section displays all 16 regions with number of achievers and probable achievers.

    1. If you tap on any one region from the list of 16 regions, you come across the list of achievers with the details of their turnover and Self Purchase along with their contact number.

    2. You can make a call to the respective direct seller in the list by tapping on the cell number displayed, right through the app- without having to enter the number in your phone book.

F] Media Library:

See, download and share all the latest offer banners, videos & photos of all the Netsurf events.



Tapping on the Media Library icon, present in middle of bottom band you can see the current offers & discounts, videos and photos from various Netsurf events.

  1. Offer/Schemes: You can download each offer banner for your own reference by tapping on the download symbol present at the bottom of every banner or you can share it via various applications like WhatsApp and Facebook by tapping on the sharing icon.

  2. Videos: You can download these views for offline display purpose and sharing. They can be downloaded and shared in the similar way mentioned above. Also, you can rate those videos with up to 5 stars.

  3. Photos: You can view different event albums here with one of their photos displayed. All the photos in that album can be seen in thumbnail view at once by tapping on ‘view all’ option. Also similarly they can be downloaded and shared with others like videos and offer/schemes.

G] Business Analytics:

Check your turnover status team wise for last phase, current phase and a forecast for the total turnover of the current phase. Also see various ways suggested to achieve the forecasted turnover.


Tapping on the Business Analytics icon which is present next to Media Library icon you can refer to various analytics done for your own business-

  1. Team wise Turnover of the current phase and the same for all the phases till date.

  2. A forecast of how much turnover can be achieved in the coming Phase.

  3. 1.You also get a customized data in 6 different ways that can be effectively used to achieve the Forecasted Turnover, like-

  1. Status Upgrade: List of probable direct sellers who can upgrade their status. Details are present with the list of all those direct sellers, their ref no.s, name, contact details and region.

  2. Missed: List of direct sellers who missed out on last Pay-out cheque. Details are present with their similar details.

  3. Repurchase: List of the direct sellers who can repurchase. Details are present with their similar details.

  4. CNRI Achievers: List of probable CNRI achiever and their details.

  5. Club Netsurf Plus Achievers: List of probable club netsurf plus achievers and their details.

  1. You can make a call to the respective direct sellers in the above mentioned lists by tapping on the cell number displayed, right through the app- without having to enter the number in your phone book.








H] Guest List:

Categorize your phone book contacts for their business prospects in to hot/warm/ cold contacts; and make important group announcements through the app.


The “Guest List” section assists you in making lists of the prospectus of your business and broadcasting messages to relevant prospects. You can now categorize the contacts in your phone in 3 broad categories based on the level of difficulty in converting them into Netsurf direct sellers, viz. Hot Contacts, Warm Contacts & Cold Contacts.



  • Hot Contacts: People who have high chances of joining the business as they have shown high interest in it.

  • Warm Contacts: These people can join the business if they find our products useful and satisfactory in terms of their requirements.

  • Cold Contacts: These people may great efforts and convincing to join the business. You may need your up line (senior) to join you while discussing the business with these prospects.



  1. After tapping on the Start Categorizing option you come across the contact list in your phone.

  2. After selecting any of the contacts, you are navigated to a new screen where in you can categorize that particular contact in the above mentioned categories.

  3. Further in every category there are following options (with abbreviations) to be selected based on the interest areas of that prospectus i.e. Health Care (HC), Personal Care (PC), Agriculture (AG), Home Care (HO) & Business (BZ). Multiple options can be selected.

  4. You can also provide the location input by tapping on Select City option at the bottom of the screen. It will lead you to Google Maps page where you can enter the area name of the contact.

  5. 1.Post completion, you can see a single contact on your screen and browse through swiping left or right or you can go back to the entire contact list view. In the list view you can find the category abbreviations for your quick reference.

  6. This app offers a feature of broadcasting a message by tapping on Broadcast Message present on the top of the screen.

  7. You will find all possible combinations of the categories on the screen. You can select any one, eg. You can select Hot Agriculture category to broadcast a message (SMS) mentioning a new Biofit product launch to all the prospects who are categorized as Hot Contact and interested in Agriculture.

  8. Sending a message is as easy as sending an SMS through your smart phone. Your one single message reaches to all the prospects in the selected category.

I] Customer Support:

Make a call to the customer support team directly through the app.



Customer support feature is user friendly and allows you to directly make a call to our customer support team with just one tap, without having to dial the actual phone no. in your phone book.




J] Settings:

Set your preferences in terms of notification timings and type of notifications you would like to receive.


The Settings option present on the upper bar helps you to manage the notifications and your interest categories, etc. in the following ways-

  1. 1.You can select the kind of alters you want to receive. Options- None, Important and All.

  2. 1.You can also select a period of the day when you would not like to receive any notifications. Say e.g. between 11 am to 6 pm.

  3. You can also manage your categories of interest by tapping on ‘More Options’ mentioned at the bottom of the page.

  4. Here, you can select from the bellows-

    1. Club Netsurf Achievers: Notifies once a direct seller from your downline achieves Club Netsurf

    2. New Content: Notifies when any new content in the media library is uploaded.

    3. New Pay-Out Cycle Commencement: Notifies when every new Pay-out cycle is commenced.

    4. New Direct Seller Login: Notifies when a new direct seller is logged in your downline

    5. Pay-out Release:Notifies when the payout is released.
